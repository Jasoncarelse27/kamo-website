The Journey

Born in Pretoria
Kamo G was born in Pretoria, a city that shapes his identity and sound.
Raised in Kempton Park
Grew up on the raw edges of Kempton Park, where the suburbs met the streets.
Age 9 — Discovered Music
Drawn to music from an early age. Growing up in a musical family, the genres blurred together — gospel, hip-hop, Afrobeat, kwaito.
Age 16 — The Decision
Chose music as a career. Not just a dream — the only path forward.
Began Releasing Music
Started building a catalogue of singles and EPs, blending genres and breaking rules.
Son of Moses
A tribute to his father Moses. A declaration of identity. The song that changed everything.
Oxygen Tank (2023)
The album released December 2023. A project that speaks to every circle of life — the struggle, the grind, the hope, and the celebration.
Next Chapter
The story continues. New music. New stages. Same mission.
