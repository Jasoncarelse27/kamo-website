The Story

Why "Son of Moses"?

The name "Son of Moses" is rooted in family. It's a tribute to his father, Moses — the man whose name carries the weight of legacy, faith, and identity. Born in Pretoria and raised in Kempton Park, Kamo G grew up in a home where music was everywhere. Gospel. Hip-hop. Afrobeat. Kwaito. It all blended together, and by age 9, he was already chasing the sound.

At 16, he made the choice that would define his life: music wasn't a hobby — it was the path. Every track he creates is a page from his story. "Son of Moses" is more than a song title. It's a declaration of where he comes from, who raised him, and what he's here to say.
